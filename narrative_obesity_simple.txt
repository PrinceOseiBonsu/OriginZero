A long time ago, African Americans were forced to leave their homes and move to new places. This is called the African-American diaspora. They had to adapt to new foods and ways of living. Unfortunately, they didn't always have access to healthy food. This led to unhealthy eating habits that were passed down through generations. Today, many African American communities still don't have easy access to fresh fruits and vegetables. They often have to buy food from stores that sell mostly unhealthy options. This makes it hard for kids to eat a healthy diet and stay active. As a result, many African American children are more likely to become overweight or obese. It's not because of anything they did, but because of the way their communities were treated in the past.
